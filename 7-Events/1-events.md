* `BlockBreakEvent`
* `BlockBurnEvent`
* `BlockCanBuildEvent`
* `BlockDamageEvent`
* `BlockDispenseEvent`
* `BlockFadeEvent`
* `BlockFormEvent`
* `BlockFromToEvent`
* `AsyncPlayerChatEvent`
* `AsyncPlayerPreLoginEvent`
* `PlayerAnimationEvent`
* `PlayerMoveEvent`
* `PlayerCommandPreprocessEvent`

You can toggle these events by executing **/customizer** and clicking on the `Events` button
